When we'll say no

  • Milliseconds matter. High-frequency trading, live media engines, and other ultra-low-latency systems belong elsewhere.
  • Native mobile at massive scale. If the core product is a mobile-first consumer app with deep device-level demands, we’ll be direct about limits.
  • Heavy compute workloads. AI inference, transcoding, or large media processing should be integrated as services, not squeezed into Bubble.
  • Strict regional hosting needs. If your compliance model demands precise hosting control, we’ll flag it before you commit.
